Our cosy studio is located in the heart of Prenzlauer Berg, at Kollwitzstr 82. We offer a flexible membership option designed for independent makers who want the freedom to create on their own schedule.

Membership includes:

  • flexible access to the studio, up to 12 hours a week
  • professional wheels - Lindemann and Shimpo Whisper-T
  • space for handbuilding
  • selection of studio glazes
  • a variety of professional tools and materials.
  • discount on studio workshops
  • space to display and sell your ceramics

*Clay and firing costs excluded.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced potter, our studio memberships offer a supportive and inspiring environment to nurture your creativity and develop your skills.

Interested in becoming a member? Contact us at claystories.berlin@gmail.com.
