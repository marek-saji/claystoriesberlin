Clay Stories is a pottery collective based in Berlin. Our goal is to create meaningful connections through the art of ceramics. We believe that every piece of clay tells a story, and we are here to help you tell yours.

Pottery courses and outdoor ceramic workshops in Berlin

Join our wheel­throwing classes, hand­building workshops, book a private session or a team event.

We also organize outdoor ceramic workshops in Berlin's beautiful parks, where you can connect with nature while creating your own pottery.

Wild clay: stories captured in clay

Clay comes in many forms, and we love to explore them all. We can teach you how to search for wild clay and make pottery with a deep connection to your local environment.
