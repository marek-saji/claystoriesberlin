Try the wheel - wheelthrowing class for beginners:

2,5 hours / 85eur per person
Private wheelthrowing class: 2,5 hours / 115 eur

This class is perfect for anyone eager to experience working on the potter’s wheel for the first time. Over 2.5 hours, you’ll be introduced to the essential techniques of wheel throwing, we’ll guide you through preparing your clay, finding balance on the wheel and shaping simple vessels.

After the class, we will take care of the next steps: bisque firing, glazing, and the final firing. You will get to choose 2 pieces that will be glazed by us in the glaze of your choice. Your pieces will be ready to pick up in about 3-4weeks.

Handbuilding workshops for beginners:

2,5 hours / 85eur per person
Private handbuilding workshop: 2,5 hours / 115 eur

In this introduction to handbuilding you will learn techniques like slab building, making pinch pots and coiling to shape your own distinctive pieces. You will also learn to experiment with surface decoration, including texturing and working with engobes, to add depth and character to your creations. Finished pieces will be glazed by us in the glaze of your choice and will be ready to pick up in about 3-4 weeks.

We invite you to explore the process with curiosity, enjoy the feel of the clay, and discover the unique pieces you are capable of making.

To book a class contact us at claystories.berlin@gmail.com


Teambuilding events, kids events and outdoor courses



Teambuilding events are a great way to foster collaboration and creativity among colleagues. We will guide your team through the process of creating unique ceramic pieces, promoting teamwork. We offer workshops both in our studio or in your chosen space, providing a unique and memorable experience for your team.

If you would like to organise a kids event in our studio, please get in touch with us for more information about available dates and pricing.
