Handbuilding workshops for beginners:

Private class: 2,5 hours / 115 eur
Class for 2 people: 2,5 hours / 100 eur per person

In this introduction to handbuilding you will learn techniques like slab building, making pinch pots and coiling to shape your own distinctive pieces. You will also learn to experiment with surface decoration, including texturing and working with engobes, to add depth and character to your creations. Finished pieces (2-3) will be glazed by us in the glaze of your choice and will be ready to pick up in about 3-4 weeks.

We invite you to explore the process with curiosity, enjoy the feel of the clay, and discover the unique pieces you are capable of making.
