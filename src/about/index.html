We're Rachel and Ania, two ceramic artists, passionate about clay and visual storytelling.

Ania Margoszczyn is a Polish ceramicist based in Berlin, creating functional works with a focus on minimalist tea ceramics. Her pieces are created to enrich daily rituals, uniting simple forms with quiet utility.

Drawing inspiration from nature and her travels, Ania incorporates processes such as woodfiring, the use of wild clay and ash glazes, while also exploring sustainable and circular approaches to ceramics.

By working with local wild clays and embracing processes that highlight the interconnectedness of making, place, and environment, she ensures that each vessel carries both individuality and a strong sense of place.
