Looking for a unique and memorable experience to share with your friends? Our clay date is the perfect way to connect, create, and have fun together.

Here's how it works:

  • Book a table for 2 people for up to 2 hours.
  • Get clay, tools and a booklet with ideas and detailed instructions of handbuilding techniques.
  • Get creative - make a cup, vase or whatever you like, and spend a cosy time with your friend.
  • Pick up your finished pieces in about 2-3 weeks - we will take care of the firing and glazing!

Booklets with instructions are available in English and German.
Please note that this is a self-guided handbuilding experience. It does not involve the use of pottery wheels.
No previous experience is neccessary.
If you would like to try wheel throwing, please check out our beginner's courses.

55 EUR per person.
*all materials and firing included. Book via email or at our studio.
